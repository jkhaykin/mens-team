About Waypoint

Waypoint is a local, not-for-profit men's community. Our focus is on men's work and being the best men we can be.

We believe that men benefit greatly from being on teams that meet regularly to support each other to live their values and achieve their dreams.

Our Values

Authentic Friendships

We support men to have authentic friendships. Sure, we like to have fun together, but we're looking for something more: authentic, lifelong friendships with great men we trust, who become like brothers.

Growth Focused

We are committed to growth. We challenge each other to be better men, and allow ourselves to be challenged. We are ruthless in our compassion.

Team Support

We support teams. Many men are on teams of 5-10 men who meet weekly. The organization meets monthly and sponsors a range of activities and online discussion groups.

Is This For You?

Come visit a monthly meeting or contact us. It's a safe, trusted environment where men have authentic relationships with other men who support each other's journey through life.
